About Me

I am Teddie Puyawan, a dedicated Multimedia Student with a strong focus on digital storytelling and visual communication. I believe in the power of media to inform, entertain, and inspire, and I am constantly honing my skills across the entire production pipeline, I am a passionate multimedia artist with experience in video editing, 3D modeling, and sound design. I possess expertise in crafting captivating visual content and narrating compelling stories for both short films and music videos. Eager to contribute creativity and technical expertise to collaborative media projects.

My coursework and personal projects have given me hands-on experience in *video editing, motion graphics, photography, and graphic design.* I am proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite and always eager to learn new tools and techniques to push creative boundaries.

I am currently looking for opportunities to apply my creativity and technical skills in a professional setting. Check out my works below!
